## 1. Site Overview
This is the personal portfolio website of **Prashanth ks** (GitHub: iamprashanthks). The site showcases his skills, projects, certificates, and technical expertise. It serves as a professional online presence for potential employers, collaborators, and clients.

## 2. Site Structure & Navigation
The portfolio is a single-page application (SPA) built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. Navigation is handled via anchor links that scroll to sections:

- **Home / Hero** – Introduction and call-to-action.
- **About** – Background, interests, and professional philosophy.
- **Tech Stack** – Icons and names of technologies used (images stored in `/tech-stack` folder).
- **Projects** – Cards with project descriptions, tech used, and links (GitHub / live demo).
- **Certificates** – Thumbnails or links to certifications (stored in `/certificates` folder).
- **Contact** – Email, social links (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.), and maybe a contact form.

Additional assets:
- `/assets` – General images, icons, or downloadable files.
- `/og-image` – Open Graph image for social media previews.
- `/scripts` – JavaScript files (including minified `scripts.min.js`).
- `styles.css` / `styles.min.css` – Main stylesheets.

## 3. Technology Stack (used to build the site)
- **HTML5** – Semantic markup.
- **CSS3** – Custom styling with responsive design (Flexbox/Grid).
- **JavaScript (ES6)** – Interactive elements (e.g., tab switching for Tech Stack, mobile menu, form validation).
- **Minification** – `styles.min.css` and `scripts.min.js` for production.
- **Hosting** – GitHub Pages (static hosting, automatic deployment from `main` branch).
